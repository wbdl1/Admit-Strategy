FAQ

Quick answers for students and parents before booking.

Is this an admissions guarantee?

No. Admit Strategy does not guarantee admission to any college. It helps students build better systems, direction, and planning habits.

Who is this for?

Motivated middle school or high school students who want stronger study habits, clearer extracurricular direction, and long-term academic planning.

How long is the free beta?

The recommended beta is 4 weeks. This is long enough to test study systems, weekly planning, parent updates, and an initial roadmap.

What is study surgery?

A live session where the student brings real schoolwork, and we fix how they study using active recall, feedback loops, and simple explanations.

Do parents get updates?

Yes. Parent visibility is part of the program, including what was done, what improved, and what should happen next.
